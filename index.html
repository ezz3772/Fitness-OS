Training Rules
1
No ego lifting. Correct form is the only rule in Month 1. Weight is secondary.
2
Progressive overload. Complete all reps easily for 2 sessions in a row → add weight next week. Not before.
3
Tempo every rep. 1 second up, 2–3 seconds down. Every. Single. Rep.
4
Rest 90 seconds between sets. Use a timer. Not 3 minutes, not 30 seconds.
5
Warm-up mandatory: 5 min bike + joint circles + 1 light set before each new muscle group.
6
No running. Knee protection phase. Incline walk or bike only for cardio.
7
Sleep target: 7 hours. Without sleep, fat loss stalls and muscle recovery fails.
8
Protein every meal. Build toward 130g/day. This protects muscle while losing fat.
9
Month 1 goal is NOT weight. Goal: complete 16 sessions, no injuries, energy improves, knee stays fine.
